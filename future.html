Biography

Nayvadius DeMun Cash mostly known as Future has been in the rap industry since 2011. He has a very unique sound and has been relevant and for the past decade every album of his have peaked at number 1 since DS2. He dropped out of College to sell drugs in his streets to people from family homes. He wanted a change then started rapping after his cousin Rico Wade persuaded him to since he was a producer. He started releasing music and got his first hit named Racks which he was a feature on it and got noticed. He really took off when he released DS2 one of his best albums which influenced many and ever since that album every album has peaked. He doesn't wear clothes more than twice and then after he goes to his old hood and gives it to the people who live there.

Notable Contributions:

  • Influenced thousands of people.
  • Collabed with people like Travis Scott, Drake, and Metro Boomin which almost all would go #1.
  • Started one of the biggest rap feuds in the industry with Kendrick Lamar and Metro Boomin against Drake.
